Services

The problem picks the tool.

Every engagement is built around one question: what is the highest value problem in this business right now? The services below are the tools.

Executive and Business Advisory

Strategy, organizational design, and executive decision support from someone who has sat in the owner's seat. Available as embedded advisory, fractional executive capacity, or defined engagements. Deliverables are executive grade: strategy memos, KPI frameworks, one page briefs, and board ready reporting.

Typical work includesRole definition and team structure, pilot program design and measurement, growth and expansion planning, and leadership reporting.

AI Implementation That Reaches Production

End to end deployment of AI inside operating companies: identifying the highest value use cases, building the systems, integrating them with existing platforms and data, and driving adoption until they are part of daily work. Includes AI adoption strategy, tool and licensing decisions, governance, and staff enablement.

Typical work includesDocument and specification review, estimating support, reporting automation, knowledge management, and operational decision support.

Financial Intelligence and Executive Reporting

Live dashboards and reporting pipelines that reconcile accounting systems with project management platforms so leadership sees one version of the truth.

Typical work includesKPI design, backlog and revenue mix analysis, margin visibility by job and status, bonding and portfolio reporting, and polished deliverables built for owners and executives.

Custom Software and Workflow Systems

Full stack internal tools built to fit the business. The process gets designed first, then the software makes it fast. Modern stack, hard data integrity rules, and documentation that lets the client's team own it.

Typical work includesCapture and intake systems, dashboards, trackers, reporting generators, and workflow automation.

Training and Workforce Development

Built for organizations that need to grow capability, not just complete training, including partnerships with labor, contractor association, and training institutions.

Typical work includesCurriculum design, certification aligned learning pathways, instructor enablement, and workforce pipeline strategy for technical trades.

Project Rescue and Revenue Recovery

When projects go sideways, structure brings them back. Forensic work on complex projects produces documented recovery packages that return outstanding cash to the business.

Typical work includesScope clarification, open item tracking, closeout planning, and forensic reconciliation of change orders and billing.

Cloud and Infrastructure Modernization

Migration planning and consolidation for internal applications, delivered as deployment plans detailed enough that an IT team can execute them without guesswork.

Typical work includesArchitecture decisions, backup and transport strategy, and cost control.
